# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and collaborate professionally.
- Focus on ideas, not individuals.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- Personal attacks or intimidation
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or reviews

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for violations.
